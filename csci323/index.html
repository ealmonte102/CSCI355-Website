Projects

You must do all of the assigned projects by yourself, copying any portion of your projects from any sources (online or offline) including getting source code from your classmates is considered cheating, as well as sharing code with you classmates
  1. 99 points will be taken away if cheating is discoveed
  2. Late projectes will be accepted only 1 day after the due date with a -1 penalty
  3. You will recieve a -m score for not submitting a soft copy of your project where m is the score assigned to that project
  4. You will receive a -m/2 score for submitting a soft copy of a project that does not compile, where m is the score assigned to that project
  5. All project submissions must follow the project submission requirements
  6. The soft copy of all projects must be submitted via email to your TA and Dr. Phillips
  7. All hard copies must be submitted at the beginning of class. Any late submission will be given a penalty of -1 points. 0 points will be given if no hard copy is submitted even if the soft copy is submitted

Attendance

Perfect attendance will be awarded +6 extra points. 1 excused absence will be awarded +3 points

Grading

The grade is computed as (X/Y) * 100 where X is the sum of all homeworks, quizzes, exams, projects and all extra credit points that you have received

Y is the total number of points (assuming a perfect grade) of all homeworks, quizzes, exams and projects.
